50 Common English Verbs

  • accept - to take or receive something offered
  • add - to join or combine two or more things
  • admire - to feel respect and approval for someone or something
  • admit - to allow someone to enter a place
  • advise - to offer suggestions about what someone should do
  • afford - to have enough money to buy or do something
  • agree - to have the same opinion as someone else
  • allow - to give permission for something to happen
  • amuse - to entertain or make someone laugh
  • analyze - to study or examine something carefully
  • announce - to make a public statement
  • annoy - to make someone angry or impatient
  • answer - to respond to a question
  • apologize - to say that you are sorry for something you did wrong
  • appear - to come into sight
  • applaud - to clap your hands to show approval
  • appreciate - to be grateful for something
